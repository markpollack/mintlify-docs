## We want projects to succeed on their own terms

At the Spring AI Community, we're committed to creating a collaborative environment that nurtures the growth and sustainability of projects and their communities.

Our guiding principles are straightforward yet impactful:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Honor project identity" icon="fingerprint">
    Respect the unique vision and goals of each project
  </Card>
  <Card title="Offer guidance, not mandates" icon="hand-holding-heart">
    Provide support while preserving project autonomy
  </Card>
  <Card title="Maintain transparency" icon="eye">
    Open communication in all our actions and decisions
  </Card>
  <Card title="Encourage long-term thinking" icon="seedling">
    Foster sustained impact and project growth
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What sets Spring AI Community apart

The Spring AI Community offers a balanced approach to governance and support, designed for the unique needs of Spring AI projects.

  <Card title="Neutral home" icon="house">
